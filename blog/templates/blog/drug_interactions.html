What Are Drug Interactions?

Drug interactions occur when one drug affects the activity of another drug when both are administered together. This can alter the effectiveness of medications.

Types of Drug Interactions

There are several types of interactions: additive, synergistic, and antagonistic. Understanding these helps prevent complications.

Common Drugs That Interact

Some common medications that interact include blood thinners, antibiotics, and pain relievers. Always consult a healthcare provider!

Symptoms of Drug Interactions

Be aware of symptoms like dizziness, increased heart rate, or unusual bleeding. If you experience these, seek medical help!
