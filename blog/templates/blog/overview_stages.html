What is Drug Development?

The drug development process transforms a promising compound into a safe and effective medication available to patients, involving multiple phases of research and testing.

Key Stages of Drug Development

1. Discovery

Identifying potential drug candidates through research and screening methods.

2. Preclinical Testing

Laboratory and animal testing to assess safety and biological activity before human trials.

3. Clinical Trials

Testing conducted in phases to evaluate safety and efficacy in human subjects.

4. FDA Review

The FDA evaluates trial data to determine whether to approve the drug for public use.
